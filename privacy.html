**Privacy Policy for FusionGrid**

Effective Date: March 30, 2025

FusionGrid ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile game available on the Google Play Store. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle user information in relation to our app.

---

**1. Personal Information Collection**

FusionGrid does not collect, use, or store any personally identifiable information directly from users.

However, we use third-party services that may collect information to provide their services. These services include, but are not limited to:

- **Google AdMob** (for displaying ads within the app)
- **Google Play Services**

These services may collect device information, advertising IDs, usage data, and cookies to personalize content and ads.

---

**2. How We Use Collected Information**

While FusionGrid itself does not collect or use your personal information, the third-party services we use may collect data to:
- Serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements
- Monitor app usage and performance
- Prevent fraud or misuse of ads

---

**3. Third-Party Services**

Please refer to the privacy policies of our third-party partners:
- [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**4. Children's Privacy**

FusionGrid is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it.
